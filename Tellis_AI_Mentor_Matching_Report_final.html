3 Priority Mentoring Areas & KPIs

Derived from call transcript with Jens Quadbeck (primary) + deal memo (secondary) — ranked by urgency and 6-month actionability. KPIs are tracking indicators only — no numerical objectives prescribed.

🎯
Area 1 — Enterprise Sales
Convert Tellis's advanced enterprise pilot conversations (Bayer, Medela, SAP) into closed annual contracts by building rigorous pain-definition, buying-center mapping, and structured large-account sales process — moving the motion beyond founder-led closing.
Jens flagged this as the top priority in the founder call: "you need to have a very, very clear and well defined pain… and you need to address a pain that's in the top three priority list of the buying center." Tellis already has enterprise interest on the table — the gap is process and qualification rigour, not lead-gen.
KPI → # enterprise meetings
🚀
Area 2 — Go-to-Market
Industrialize the transition from hands-on manual pilots (food/supplements vertical) to a scalable launch plan with crisp ICP definition, repeatable outbound engine, packaging of the Starter / Growth / Scale tiers, and vertical-by-vertical expansion sequencing (food → furniture → electronics).
Founder Artem stated in the call: "we're, you know, doubling down on go to market… we need to get more technical resources and additional engineering and design team, and then, you know, scale to larger retailers." 10% of the raise is earmarked specifically for GTM (account-based marketing + agency partnerships).
KPI → % MRR growth
🌎
Area 3 — International Scaling
Open the US mid-market for Tellis — leveraging higher purchasing power, faster agentic-commerce technology adoption, and a more receptive enterprise-buyer mindset than UK / EU brands — through US-based mentor relationships, channel partners, and an entry sequencing plan.
Artem in the call: "US is a big market for us… high purchasing power, both from consumers and businesses… technology for agentic commerce is more advanced there." US expansion is funded directly via the £200K remaining tranche of the round and identified as the next phase post-launch.
KPI → % revenue abroad
